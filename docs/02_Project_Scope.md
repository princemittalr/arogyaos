# Project Scope Document

Version: 1.0

# Overview

ArogyaOS is an AI-powered Unified Healthcare Operating System designed to digitize and intelligently manage healthcare facilities ranging from Primary Health Centres (PHCs) and Community Health Centres (CHCs) to private hospitals and clinics.

The platform provides a centralized ecosystem connecting citizens, healthcare providers, pharmacies, laboratories, hospital administrators, and district health authorities through a role-based cloud platform enhanced with Artificial Intelligence.

The primary objective is to improve operational efficiency, eliminate manual processes, optimize healthcare resources, and enable proactive decision-making using predictive analytics.

---

# Project Objectives

The project aims to:

- Digitize healthcare facility operations.
- Provide real-time operational visibility.
- Predict medicine shortages before they occur.
- Forecast patient demand.
- Improve doctor and staff management.
- Monitor laboratory services.
- Optimize bed utilization.
- Enable district-level healthcare monitoring.
- Improve citizen access to healthcare services.
- Support multilingual communication.
- Deliver AI-powered recommendations for healthcare administrators.

## 10. AI Modules

### AI Stock Prediction

Predict medicine shortages based on historical usage and patient trends.

### AI Patient Forecast

Forecast daily, weekly and monthly patient inflow.

### AI Resource Redistribution

Recommend transfer of medicines, equipment or staff between nearby facilities.

### AI Hospital Health Score

Generate an overall operational health score using multiple performance indicators.

### AI Alert Engine

Generate alerts for:

- Medicine shortages
- Doctor absenteeism
- High patient load
- Bed shortages
- Equipment failures

### AI Voice Assistant

Convert doctor voice input into structured consultation notes.

### AI Analytics

Generate intelligent summaries and operational insights for administrators.

# Out of Scope (Hackathon Version)

The following features are intentionally excluded from the MVP to maintain focus and ensure a polished implementation:

- Billing & Payments
- Insurance Claim Processing
- Payroll & HR
- Supplier Procurement
- Blood Bank Management
- Ambulance GPS Tracking
- Telemedicine Video Calls
- Wearable Device Integration
- National Health Record Integration (ABHA)
- Advanced Clinical Decision Support
- AI Disease Diagnosis
- Accounting & Finance
- Multi-Hospital Corporate Management

These may be included in future versions.
