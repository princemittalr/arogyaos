# AI Features

Version: 1.0

---

# Overview

Artificial Intelligence is the core of ArogyaOS.

Instead of merely storing healthcare data, ArogyaOS continuously analyzes operational information to predict future events, recommend corrective actions, detect anomalies, and assist healthcare professionals and district administrators in making informed decisions.

The AI layer is powered by Google Gemini and operates across every module of the platform.

---

# AI Objectives

The AI system aims to

- Predict shortages before they occur
- Improve healthcare resource utilization
- Reduce operational delays
- Assist doctors during consultations
- Improve patient experience
- Support district-level healthcare planning
- Generate actionable recommendations instead of only displaying data
